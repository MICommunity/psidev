Proteomics Standards Initiative

Satellite meeting to

HUPO world congress 2003, Montreal

Thursday, October 9, 2003

Preliminary agenda

Current PSI initiatives and related projects will be presented during the PSI abstract session, which is part of the conference programme. Please refer to the conference website for the abstract session program.
The abstract session will be followed by a PSI discussion session of 1.5 hours. The discussion session will be split into the three work groups protein interactions, mass spectrometry, and proteomics data integration.
We have collected the following agenda items so far, please send any additions or corrections to hhe@ebi.ac.uk.

Mass spectrometry work group
Chair: Randall Julian, Weimin Zhu

  • Demonstation of the Mz XML format and support applications
  • Discussion of format and support applications
  • Controlled vocabularies for mass spectrometry parameters
  • Outline plans for search engine output format specification
  • Next steps

Protein interactions work group
Chair: Henning Hermjakob

  • Format development: Discussion of objectives for level 2
  • Process formalisation: Maintenance of controlled vocabularies
  • Data exchange of user submitted data between PSI partners
  • Coordination of APIs
  • Collaboration with journals

Proteomics data integration work group
Chair: Rolf Apweiler

  • Standardisation of formats across HUPO tissue initiatives
  • Collaboration with MGED consortium

Henning Hermjakob, hhe@ebi.ac.uk, September 18, 2003
