Introduction

The HUPO world congress 2004 takes place in Beijing, October 25 - 27, 2004. Prior to the congress, October 23-24, there are satellite meetings of the HUPO work groups, one of them the Proteomics Standards Initiative. This meeting will present the current status of PSI in the different domains, and will be a forum to present our work to a broader community and to get community feedback.

The preliminary agenda is presented below, details about locations and logistics will be added later. The meeting is open to all participants, not only current contributors to the Proteomics Standards Initiative. Please feel free to suggest additional discussion items!
