The PSI Ontology


Backgound

The HUPO PSI General Proteomics Standards (GPS) and Mass Spectrometry (MS) working groups are in the process of building the ontology that will support all formats generated by the various PSI projects. The ontology will not however be tied to the use of PSI formats, as this is an unecessary restriction on its utility. Ultimately the PSI ontology will be rolled into the MGED Ontology, under the PSI namespace.

 

The ontology in its current state

Currently the ontology itself has not been created. We are in the first instance compiling terms (with accession numbers) to directly support the mzData format. This 'derived' list of terms is at version 0.0. It can be browsed as HTML here, or downloaded as an Excel spreadsheet here.

The accession numbers applied to these terms will persist, even if the terms are superceded (deprecated).

Source documents can be browsed (for inspiration) in here.

 

Referring to the ontology from mzData's 'cvLookupType' element

<cvLookupType cvLabel="PSI" name="The PSI Ontology" version="0.0" address="http://psidev.sourceforge.net/ontology/">
