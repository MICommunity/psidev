HUPO conference 2004, Beijing

PSI-MS report

October 23-24, 2004

Introduction

The HUPO world congress 2004 took place in Beijing, October 25 - 27, 2004. Prior to the congress, October 23-24, there were satellite meetings of the HUPO work groups, one of them the Proteomics Standards Initiative (PSI). The PSI-meeting presented the current status of PSI in the different domains
